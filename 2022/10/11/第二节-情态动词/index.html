情态动词

一、含义

本身有含义，表示情绪及状态

二、特点

不能单独做位于，后面必须加动词原型，没有数的变化

三、基本用法

1、can—could能够，可以

1.1、表示个人能力

eg: You can take a house to the water, but you can’t make him drink.强扭的瓜不甜

1.2、表示惊讶、怀疑、不相信的态度(不可能)，用于否定/疑问句

eg: How can this be true.怎么能是真的呢

1.3、补充：常用搭配：can’t…too…再…也不为过

eg：The handsome boy can't have too many girlfriend.

2、may—might可能可以

2.1、否定形式：may not可能不/can not不可能

eg: He may not say some sweet words,but he really loves you.

2 .2、常用搭配：may/might as well do还是…的好/不妨

eg: There’s nothing to do, then I may as well go to bed.

2.3、表示祝愿：

eg: May you success. / May the force be with you.愿原力与你同在

3、must：主观上必须–mustn’t禁止 / have to ：客观上不得不

eg: I ``mustpass the exam , or I willhave to``` apply for a job.

以Must开头的疑问句及其回答

Must I learn English?

—Yes,you must.

—No, you needn't. / No, you don't have to.

4、ought to = should应该

补充：shall：

4.1、用于第一人称，表示将要做某事

4.2、用于二三人称，表示命令、法律、宣言、警告

eg：According to local laws, you shall leave in 3 days.

5、will—would

5.1、will

v.将要（表将来） eg：They will have a picnic.野餐

n.意志、意愿 eg：Where there is a will, there is a way.有志者事竟成

5.2、would

v.就会

用于疑问词表示请求 eg：Would you marry me?

用于过去将来时

eg：I will be a father soon

eg：I didn't know I would be a father soon

6、need

6.1、n.需求 in need of

eg：I’m in need of money.

6.2、实义动词

主语是人need to do

eg：I need to make money.

主语是物 need doing / need to be done

eg：The car needing pepairing.

四、特殊用法

1、表示对事物的推测

1.1、对于现在、将来的推测

must do / be(是)

can’t / couldn’t do / be

may / might

1.2、对于过去事实的猜测

肯定：must have done

否定：can’t have done

可能：may / might have done

2、表示责备、批评

2.1、本应该做…但实际没做

ought to / should have done

eg：He should have studied hard.

2.2、本不应该做…却做了

should not have done

eg：You should not to have gone swimming to the river.

2.3、本没有必要做…但实际做了

needn’t have done

eg：I needn't have depended on my talent(才华), because I’m handsome.
